Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Amman, Oud & Oak brings together the rich culinary heritage of the Levant and contemporary techniques. Every dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients and a passion for storytelling through flavor.

Our chefs, trained in Paris and Beirut, create a menu that changes with the seasons — always fresh, always surprising. The name reflects the warmth of oud (wood) and the strength of oak, symbolizing our commitment to tradition and innovation.

Since opening in 2018, we have become a destination for those seeking an unforgettable dining experience. Whether it's a romantic dinner or a family celebration, Oud & Oak offers an ambiance that is both elegant and welcoming.

Meet Chef Karim Nasser

Chef Karim Nasser trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and worked in Michelin-starred kitchens before returning to his homeland. His philosophy: respect tradition, but never stop innovating.

"I want people to taste Jordan in every bite. Whether it's the smoky freekeh or the wild za'atar from the hills, our land tells a story."

— Karim Nasser
