Our Philosophy

Born from Passion,
Refined by Craft

Oud & Oak was born from a simple belief — that dining should be a ceremony. Each dish is a conversation between ancient Levantine tradition and modern culinary artistry.

Our chefs source only from small local farms, creating menus that change with the seasons and honour the land that feeds us. Every plate tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and love.

Our Story

Where every dish has a memory

The Beginning

A Passion for Levantine Heritage

Oud & Oak was founded in 2013 by Chef Nasser Al-Amin, a Jordanian chef who trained in Paris before returning to Amman with a singular mission — to elevate the cuisine he grew up eating into something extraordinary.

Every recipe draws from generations of Levantine knowledge, reinterpreted through the lens of modern technique. We use only what the Jordanian landscape provides — olive oil from Ajloun, lamb from Wadi Rum, herbs from the Jordan Valley.
