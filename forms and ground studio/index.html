The Studio

Built on Honesty of Material

Form & Ground was founded in Amman in 2011 with a single conviction — that great architecture does not impose itself on a place, but emerges from it. We work with concrete, stone, and timber as they are, without apology.

Our projects range from private residences to civic infrastructure, always with the same rigorous attention to how light moves through a space, how a threshold is crossed, how a building meets the ground.
